ACTION & CUT


Description


Be ready to unleash your imagination and share your unique stories as motion picture in Our Big Stage. “ACTION & CUT” is the perfect platform for emerging filmmakers , storytellers and Cinematographers around us where the budding filmmakers showcase their talent and creativity through their short film.



Rules and Regulations


▶ Short films, documentaries, the essence of nature, and music videos are strongly encouraged, with a minimum duration of 3 minutes to 10 minutes.

▶ The deadline for submission is on or before March 31st. Late submissions will not be considered.

▶ The decision of the judges is final. Participation in the event constitutes acceptance of the rules and regulations.

▶ Important Note: Censored content is strictly prohibited.
