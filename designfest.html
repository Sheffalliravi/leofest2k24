DESIGN FIESTA


Description


Welcome to the Design Fest, where creativity meets functionality in UI/UX design! Participants will tackle real-world problems by crafting innovative designs using Figma. Showcase your skills in visual clarity, color theory, and design system implementation to solve the given problem statement.


Rules and Regulations


▶ Individual Participation

▶ Focused on UI/UX design using Figma.

▶ Participants will receive the problem statement on the event day and have a designated time to complete their designs.

▶ Designs must address the provided problem statement and adhere to the principles of good UI/UX design.

▶ Judging criteria include neatness, clarity of presentation, effective use of colors, and the implementation of design systems.

▶ Plagiarism or the use of pre-made templates will result in disqualification.
