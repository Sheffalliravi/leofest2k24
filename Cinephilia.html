CINIPHILIA


Description


Cinephillia is an event designed to test participants' knowledge and passion for movies. Through three rounds of challenges, teams will compete by identifying movie names, songs, and dialogues.


RULES AND REGULATIONS


▶ Team size: 1 - 2.

▶ Only one answer per question is allowed.

▶ Teams must refrain from using any external resources or devices during the competition.

▶ Any form of cheating or unsportsmanlike behavior will result in immediate disqualification.

▶ The decisions of the judges will be final and binding. ▶ Only team leaders should complete the event registration form.
