General Instruction


▶ Under team registration, two members are allowed.


Line Follower Race


Rules and Regulations


▶ The size of the robot shall not exceed 20cm (length) x 20cm (width) x 20cm (height).

▶ The robot must move autonomously and start with the push of a button. Any wireless or wired remote control is not allowed.

▶ The robot will be placed at one end of the track, and the time taken to reach the finish line will be measured.

▶ The robot that reaches the finish line faster is considered the winner.

▶ The electric voltage anywhere in the machine must not exceed 12V DC at all times for each robot.



Maze Solver


Rules and Regulations


▶ The MicroMouse shall not jump over, fly over, climb, scratch, cut, burn, mark, damage, or destroy the walls of the maze.

▶ The size of the MicroMouse shall not exceed 17cm (height) * 17cm (width).

▶ The MicroMouse should not exceed a maximum weight of 2kg.

▶ It must be controlled autonomously with no human aid, and a MicroMouse shall not use an energy source employing a combustion process.

▶ The robot must be powered by a power source such as a battery fixed on the robot; it cannot be powered by a stationary power source connected to the robot by a cord.

▶ Two penalties will be applied: a search penalty, indicating the time spent on the maze to start a new run, and a touch penalty, reflecting how many times the MicroMouse touched the walls.

▶ A runtime is recorded after every run of the mouse, covering the distance from the starting position to the destination.
