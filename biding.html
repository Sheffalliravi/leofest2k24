BIDDING BASH


Description


Join us for the ultimate cricket frenzy at our IPL Auction event! Experience the excitement of bidding for your favorite players, strategizing with your team, and competing to build the ultimate squad. Don't miss this chance to showcase your cricket knowledge and negotiation skills in a thrilling atmosphere filled with fellow college enthusiasts.!


Rules and Regulations


▶ Team size: 1 - 3.

▶ Bidding for each player will be open for 10 seconds only, with bidding closing automatically once the timer expires or if no further bids are made.

▶ Each team will be allocated a virtual budget for player bidding.

▶ Teams cannot exceed their allocated budget during bidding.

▶ The decision of the event organizers regarding player auctions and team compositions will be final.

▶ The IPL team name will be chosen at random from a bowl during the event.

▶ Only team leaders should complete the event registration form.
