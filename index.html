About Us




Leo Fest is a National Level Symposium organized for College Students. This includes both Technical and Non-Technical events. The events are planned in a way which helps an individual to increase their self-confidence and to overcome their stage fear. "Experience is the best teacher everyone could afford". The experience gained by this event can help you learn a lot. We expect everyone's participation in this glorious event...!!!!!
